SAFe Scrum Master

December 5-6, 2019
Learn More

As the demand for SAFe expertise accelerates globally, there is a growing focus on the Scrum Master and the critical role that person plays in a SAFe implementation. The SSM credential is a must have certification for Scrum Masters looking to boost their value in today’s career marketplace.

Unlike traditional Scrum Master training that focuses on the fundamentals of team-level Scrum, the Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) Scrum Master course explores the role of the Scrum Master in the context of the entire enterprise.

What's Inlcuded:

  • Attendee Workbook
  • Course certificate
  • Catered breakfast and lunch
  • Eligibility to take the SAFe® 4 Scrum Master (SSM) exam
  • One year membership to the SAFe Community Platform

SAFe POPM

December 5-9, 2019
Learn More

SAFe is improving business outcomes for companies across the world. Delivering maximum business value amid digital disruption and getting the business results needed is what SAFe Product Owners and Product Managers do through effective Program Increment execution.

Learn the skillsets needed to guide the delivery of value in a Lean enterprise and the activities, tools, and mechanics used to manage backlogs and programs.

What's Inlcuded:

  • Attendee Workbook
  • Course certificate
  • Catered breakfast and lunch

  • One year membership to the SAFe Community Platform

Agile Fundamentals

January 23-24, 2020
Learn More

The two-day Agile Fundamentals course emphasizes starting with an Agile mindset, not a single methodology or framework. Course participants come away with a solid understanding of core concepts as they prepare to embark on their agile journey.

For those who want a good introduction to some widely applied Agile frameworks and practices. Participants will get a solid understanding of the processes and the environment of an agile project, and the tools and techniques used in delivering value the agile way.

What's Inlcuded:

  • Attendee Workbook
  • Course certificate
  • Catered breakfast and lunch
  • Awarded Agile Fundamentals (ICP) Certification
  • ICP is the first certification on the ICAgile Roadmap
